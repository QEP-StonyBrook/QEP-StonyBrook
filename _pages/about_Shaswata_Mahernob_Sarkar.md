### Shaswata Mahernob Sarkar

Shaswata Mahernob Sarkar is a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at Stony Brook University, having started in Fall 2025. He earned his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering in 2024 from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). His undergraduate thesis, titled _Enhanced Quantum Convolutional Neural Networks (QCNNs) with Effective Feature Processing Strategies for Image Classification_, explored leveraging quantum computing principles to enhance classical problem-solving capabilities. His current research focuses on integrated quantum information processor.
